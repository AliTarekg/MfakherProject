Mafakher is a leading organization in the field of organizing exhibitions, conferences, parties, and events. It has adopted, to achieve its excellence, a team of experienced and competent professionals in the administrative and technical aspects specialized in organizing the largest exhibitions and conferences for ministries, authorities, governmental and institutional bodies. This is achieved by committing to the highest levels of quality in providing all organizational services, adhering to innovative technical principles starting from preparations, marketing organizing programs, attracting participants, marketing and sales services, and preparing exhibition spaces to meet the expectations of the organizing entities, participants, and visitors.
